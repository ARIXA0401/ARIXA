Clinical Intelligence

Not Just a Blood Test. A Complete Clinical Picture.

Every blood panel processed through our London Medical Laboratory integration returns more than numbers. ARIXA delivers a comprehensive clinical intelligence dashboard — reviewed by a named clinician — that transforms raw biomarker data into actionable clinical insight.

Summary

A clinician-reviewed narrative of the patient's results, highlighting the most significant findings, key trends since the previous test, and an overall health score. Weight loss progress tracked alongside metabolic markers for a holistic view.

Results

Every biomarker displayed with visual range indicators showing exactly where the patient sits relative to reference ranges. Side-by-side comparison with previous tests shows the direction of travel — with percentage changes flagged for easy clinical review.

Patterns

AI-powered cross-panel analysis that identifies clinical patterns invisible when markers are viewed individually. For example: connecting liver enzyme elevations with iron markers to suggest a unified clinical picture. Each pattern includes a plain-language explanation and a recommended next step.

Actions

Prioritised clinical recommendations ranked by urgency: Urgent (within 48 hours), Important (within 2 weeks), Follow Up (4–6 weeks), and Lifestyle (ongoing). Each action includes a clear explanation and what the patient should discuss with their prescriber.

The Structural Gap

What Happens Between Inspections?

Most healthcare organisations maintain documented procedures and protocols. Yet between statutory reviews, a structural gap emerges: what's documented and what actually happens diverge.

Procedural drift occurs silently. Undetected deviations accumulate, regulatory risk grows, and the next review can reveal gaps nobody saw coming.

This isn't negligence — it's the natural consequence of running complex operations without continuous oversight of governance adherence. ARIXA bridges that gap.
